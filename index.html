About Us

Located in Houston, TX

Lens Photo & Video offers a variety of photography and cinematic videography services. Whether you are looking for engagement photographer or a wedding photographer, we’re here to serve you and capture those precious memories. If you want a photographer at your location, we’ll come to you, whether that be at your home or at your business.


If you’re looking for creative studio shots, we can schedule your photo shoot to take place within a rented photography studio in the Houston/Sugar land region or we can bring a portable studio with professional lighting to your location of choice.​
